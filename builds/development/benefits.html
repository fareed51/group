BENEFITS OF AXELERATOR

Axelerator is built for dynamic insurance businesses. Its unique design supports
rapid implementation and regular updates.

Axelerator| Benefits| Components

DESIGNED FOR YOUR BUSINESS

Axelerator will save you time (and money) in administering policies and processing claims.

Our focus is on increasing efficiency in all aspects of software development and business processing. We continually improve Axelerator’s tools, technology and procedures to reduce complexity for our insurer clients.

With Axelerator you can:

Improve business efficiency

Streamline policy and claims processes

Eliminate paper processing

Automate routine tasks

Reduce duplication

Focus attention on exceptions

Target new business opportunities.

Enabling customer self-service

Your customers will experience an instant turnaround by having direct on-demand access to their policies and claims. At the same time, administrative staff will be less distracted by customer queries.

Consistent, better service

The rules engine will apply your business rules consistently. This means staff are freed up to focus on complex issues.

Rapid response

95% of Axelerator tailoring is completed through configuration, not coding. With this rapid development capability, you can make prompt adjustments to market changes. Further, data analysis tools let you monitor your changes and identify further areas for improvement.

Progressive roll out

There is no need to “rip out” and replace your existing systems in one go. Axelerator can overlay your existing legacy systems, allowing you to replace the back end progressively, while presenting a modern front-end view to your customer.

Flexible technology platform

Axelerator is built on current Java frameworks to open standards to facilitate integration. We continuously update it as better technology becomes available.
