OUR WORK

Axe has successfully completed hundreds of system development projects across
Australia and New Zealand for insurer clients.
Find out how we work, and read our case studies below.

We work with you – when you want, how you want.

When you engage us for your important work, we will always:

work collaboratively with you

adhere to your scope and delivery dates

break the project into clear tasks

develop comprehensive automated test scripts

deliver regular drops for testing (usually every two to three weeks)

keep you updated on time and materials charges.

You’re always in control

We work on the basis of an uncapped scope, and time and material charging so that you always have the flexibility to make changes as you need.

Our approach is agile, adaptable, and you avoid the risks and potential budget-blowouts of a structured “waterfall” approach.

EMERGENCY

Urgent launch of new campaign to increase sales

Our client had multiple projects running sequentially for the same business area, and within the same code base. The client wanted to urgently launch a new marketing campaign ahead of the other in-flight projects.

Our agile approach meant we could deal immediately with the client’s new objective. We minimised disruption by quickly re-ordering the timing of the planned releases to fit in the release of the new marketing campaign.

Our team put in extra hours to successfully deliver the campaign changes on time, and then smoothly resumed the original projects.

We did not waste our client’s time with processing change requests, estimating the impact of rescheduling releases and the costs of backing out changes and adding them back later.

TACTICAL

New Business system for a super fund

Our client won a deal with a new super fund, and had an extremely tight deadline to provide an online new business system. To successfully deliver the project we:

started the project immediately, even though it was just four days before Christmas

commenced before a Statement of Work could be signed

provided project estimates and plans within two days

allowed for regular changes in requirements to accommodate the super fund’s requests as they reviewed the work in progress.

The Axe team delivered a robust new business system, integrated with our client’s existing funds system, within just four months. Our client was able to go live by the deadline, and avoided the losses associated with delays.

STRATEGIC

Replacing back office legacy systems

Forward-thinking clients trust us with the often challenging task of replacing their out-dated and inefficient systems.

We competently develop systems for back-end processing, and smoothly transition our clients through the process of replacing deeply embedded software and procedures.

These clients are now enjoying the efficiencies of streamlined processing, as well as other benefits of the new technology – including access to continuous platform improvements.
